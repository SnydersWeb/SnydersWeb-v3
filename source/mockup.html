Welcome to my website. This site requires Internet Explorer 5.5 or higher, or any browser that is W3C DOM compliant such as Mozilla 1.0 or Netscape 7.

Mozilla is still under development, builds prior to 0.9.5 may have difficulty correctly processing this website.

This website is divided up into several content areas:
  • About Me - tells a little more about me, what I do and like. It also contains my résumé.
  • Web Sites - a comprehensive portfolio of all the websites I have created for my previous employer and personally.
  • Art Portfolio - any artwork I created outside of web sites. Shows off most of the projects I produced to earn my degree from Purdue University.
  • Destinations - a gallery of photographs I have taken from my various trips.
  • Contact Me - call me, email me, or mail me.
If you would like to know more about this web site, please have a look at my Personal Web Sites page.
